Consulting

A disciplined four-step loop.

We begin with a question and follow a disciplined four-step process — from diagnosis to decision. After Debrief, clients return to Discover with a sharper question.

01

Discover Why

What is the real problem or opportunity? A fact-based definition before any solution is proposed.

02

Design How

What does the right solution look like for your specific context?

03

Deliver What

Executing with precision, alongside your team, measuring against the objectives.

04

Debrief What next

Compare results to intent. Exit with confidence — or return to Discover.

In Practice

Global Engineering Firm — Project Controls

Discover

The stated problem was a skills gap. The real problem was behavioural — teams had the tools but lacked a shared model for why planning discipline matters.

Design

A single governing principle shaped everything: a weak Work Breakdown Structure makes forecasting impossible. That theory determined the workshop design.

Deliver

Three sessions across North American time zones. Teams applied the principle to their own projects, their own WBS gaps, their own forecasting challenges.

Debrief

Client returned to Discover twice, each time with a sharper question — first trust between PCs and PMs, then making the value of Project Controls visible.

93.3% of participants said they would recommend this program.
